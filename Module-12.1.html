History of the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, was commissioned by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. This magnificent mausoleum, built between 1632 and 1653, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of eternal love.

Architectural Marvel

The Taj Mahal is renowned for its symmetrical design and intricate marble inlay work. The stunning white marble changes color depending on the time of day and light, adding to its allure.
